Hi, I'm Seungwoo Kang.

I am a final-year undergraduate student at Hongik University, studying Computer Engineering. I am currently a research intern at SCAI Lab, Chung-Ang University. My research focuses on speech processing and large language models — I am particularly interested in how machines can understand and generate human speech in ways that feel natural and contextually aware. Outside the lab, I have been playing guitar and composing for over 6 years.

My research interests include:

  • Speech Processing
  • Knowledge Distillation
  • Mechanistic Interpretability
